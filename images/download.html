    Global HR Community brings together HR professionals, People Managers, Corporate Organisations, HR Service Providers, Entrepreneurs, Academia, Faculties & Students  for sharing knowledge, fostering innovation, and promoting wholistic development and growth through our guiding principle : Connect, Learn, Grow.

    Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

    01What is GHRC?

    +

    GHRC, or Global HR Community, is an online platform dedicated to connecting HR professionals, students, academicians, and thought leaders from around the world. It offers a range of resources and opportunities for learning, networking, and professional growth in the field of Human Resources.

    02How can I join GHRC?

    +

    Joining GHRC is simple. Visit our website, click on the ‘Join Now’ button, and fill out the registration form. We offer various membership options to cater to different needs and interests.

    03 Can we contribute to the Blogs & Training Courses

    +

    Yes. We encourage our members to contribute their Articles, Research Papers, Case Studies, News to the Library. We also accept Training Courses and list them on a revenue sharing model.

    04 What types of courses does GHRC Academy offer?

    +

    GHRC Academy offers a wide range of courses covering various HR topics, from talent management and recruitment strategies to HR analytics and leadership. These courses are designed for all levels, from beginners to experienced professionals.

    05 What is the HR Marketplace?

    +

    The HR Marketplace is a feature where members can find and offer HR-related products and services. It includes tools, software, consultation services, and much more.

    06 Can I list my HR services on GHRC?

    +

    Yes, GHRC has a dedicated section for HR Service Providers. You can list your services by signing up for a provider account and adding your business details to our directory.

    07 What is the gamification system in GHRC?

    +

    GHRC’s gamification system encourages member engagement by awarding points and badges for participation in community activities. Members can earn rewards and recognition, enhancing their learning and networking experience.

    08 How can I get involved in GHRCs forums and discussions?

    +

    Once you become a member, you can participate in forums and discussions by navigating to the ‘Community’ section. Here, you can join conversations, share insights, and connect with other HR professionals.

    09 Are there opportunities for networking within GHRC?

    +

    Yes, GHRC offers numerous networking opportunities through virtual events, webinars, and forums. Members can connect with peers, mentors, and industry leaders globally.
